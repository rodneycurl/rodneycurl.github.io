Here's a little about me

    For over 10 years, Rodney has been at the intersection of music and technology by creating innovative, brand experiences across digital, social, print, and experiential mediums. He has had the pleasure of collaborating with notable brands like Audi, Coca Cola, Delta Airlines, Dick’s Sporting Goods, Essentia Water, Fender, Georgetown University, Hilton, HP, Hyundai, Kia, Levi’s, Live Nation, Nabisco, Nike, Old Spice, SCJohnson, Target, Volkswagen, Wieden+Kennedy, and many more.

    Currently, Rodney is the Founder and Brand Strategist at Haverstack and Company, a full-service brand management agency specializing in strategy and digital media.
