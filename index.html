About

Rodney Curl is a keen brand marketing manager, digital analyst, marketer, and entrepreneur. He graduated from Howard University in 2009 with a Bachelor’s degree in Journalism before founding Stereo Champions, a digital media company based in Washington, DC that produces original content about new music and live events. In addition to his work at Stereo Champions, Curl has over 8 years of experience in the advertising industry, helping to create successful campaigns for notable multi-million dollar brands such as Nike, Coca-Cola, Levi’s, Audi, and Delta Airlines. He’s held positions at Wieden+Kennedy, Deloitte, AKQA, Georgetown University and successfully launched an iPhone app that achieved over 2.5M downloads worldwide. Curl is a proud husband that enjoys spending time listening to music, DJing with his wife and watching Toy Story.
