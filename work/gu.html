DESCRIPTION

Georgetown University Football needed a fun video to promote the upcoming football games for the year. After watching a few games on Sunday Night and coordinating with the Marketing Director and Jack (Mascot) we decided to produce a short video promotion showcasing all of the best touchdown celebrations from the weekend. Many of the celebrations includes Cam Newton's famous Superman pose, Victor Cruz notorious cha-cha dance, Antonio Brown's twerk moves, and more.
