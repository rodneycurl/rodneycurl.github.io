PROJECT

Georgetown University Football →

ROLE

Strategist

CHALLENGE

Produce an online marketing campaign to excite Georgetown fans and alumni for the upcoming football season.

KEY INSIGHTS

Georgetown football is not the SEC when it comes to football. Students and alumni need a call to action to bring them to the stadium. When you think about Georgetown University sports the first few things that comes to mind is John Thompson Jr., Patrick Ewing, Alonzo Mourning, Allen Iverson, and simply put division one basketball. So to push for the football teams chance to get into the spotlight we teammed up with their mascot Jack to do some of the most popular NFL enzone dance celebrations to get Hoyas excited for the season.
