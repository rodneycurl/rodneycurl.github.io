PROJECT

Georgetown University Football →

ROLE

Marketer, Strategist, Videographer, and Editor

OBJECTIVE

Produce an online marketing campaign to excite Georgetown fans and alumni for the upcoming football season

STRATEGY

The goal with this project was to get Georgetown University Football fans ready for the season. When you think about Georgetown University sports the first few things that comes to mind is John Thompson Jr., Patrick Ewing, Alonzo Mourning, Allen Iverson, and simply put division one basketball. So to push for the football teams chance to get into the spotlight we teammed up with Jack to do some of the most popular NFL enzone dance celebrations.

RESULTS 

Reach: 26K+, Video Views: 4.2K+
