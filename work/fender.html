PROJECT

Fender Urban Strategy →

ROLE

Strategist

CHALLENGE

Elevate and amplify the Fender brand within the Urban music space.

KEY INSIGHTS

The ultimate goal is to empower Urban music artists with the gift of Fender. Many urban artist have never touched a guitar yet along learned to play one. Every musician is creative some just need access to the tools to explore them. This will create the future generation of great musicians.

RESULTS 

To get as many Fender guitars and basses in the hands of the Urban music talent. By producing a series of activations, supporting tours, providing supreme artist relations, and identifying the talent within urban music. Leading to millions of earned media impressions.

Press: Billboard, Guitar Center, Complex, and more.
