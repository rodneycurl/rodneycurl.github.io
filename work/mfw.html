PROJECT

MyFaceWhen App →

ROLE

Strategist

CHALLENGE

How do you get mobile users excited about a GIF making app they will want to share with the world.

KEY INSIGHTS

Many millennials were constantly utilizing their mobile devices and always on their laptops. We found that this group enjoys meme culture and needed an outlet that made it easy for them to send their friends GIFs to describe their feelings whether at the office, party, or a music festival. This led us to create demos onsite at music festivals, building relationships with influencers for the initial beta release, partnering with an international app platforms to give MFW away for free, and landing a spot on Dallas News CW33 as the App of the Week.

RESULTS 

Downloads: 500K+
Apple Store Rating: 4.5
Reach: 500+ Countries
Press: Mashable, Gizmodo, CNet, CW33 News, and more.
