PROJECT

Allen Iverson Hall Of Fame →

CHALLENGE

Spread the word, commemorate and amplify Allen Iverson's NBA Hall of Fame Induction.

KEY INSIGHTS

Allen Iverson is one of the most electrifying basketball players to ever play the game. At Georgetown University he was and still is a legend. Fans wanted to stay informed and also have a piece of history to walk away with that honored The Answer. So we developed a customized Bobblehead of him to give out to the first 5K fans to come to the home game the week of his induction, sent bobbleheads to influencers, gave away Iverson drawstring backpacks, and built out an entire social media strategy to keep fans and alum engaged up to the very moment that Iverson was inducted into the National Basketball Association Hall Of Fame.

RESULTS 

Reach: 610K+, Clicks: 45K+, Video Views: 82K+, Shares: 1.8K+, Press: Washington Post, ESPN's Mike And Mike
