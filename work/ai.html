PROJECT

Allen Iverson Hall Of Fame →

CHALLENGE

We need to spread the word, commemorate and amplify Allen Iverson's NBA Hall of Fame Induction to students, alumni, and fans of Georgetown University.

KEY INSIGHT & STRATEGY

People love to celebrate big events with tangible gifts and awards. Keep fans excited and give them a way to take home a piece of Allen Iverson’s Hall of Fame history. We developed a limited edition Bobblehead of him to give out to the first 2K fans and 500 students to come to the home game the week of his induction, sent bobbleheads to influencers, gave away Iverson Georgetown themed #3 drawstring backpacks, and built out an entire social media strategy using Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram to keep fans and alum engaged up to the very moment that Iverson was inducted into the NBA Hall Of Fame.

RESULTS 

% of Tickets Sold: 100% (The game was sold out)

Social Media Reach: 610K+

Social Media Video Views: 82K+

Social Media Shares: 1.8K+

Press: Washington Post and ESPN's Mike And Mike
