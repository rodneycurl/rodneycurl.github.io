ROLE

DESCRIPTION

Vote Funny is a website that gives comedy fans the power to add their favorite comedy shows to the website and also vote on which ones are the funniest. So at any given time you can visit Vote Funny and find out which show is the most hilarious each week. Think of it as having the opportunity to get the pulse of what you should really be laughing at in your spare time.
