CHALLENGE

Stereo Champions is a music media website I put together to highlight music and live events. It's a fun passion project that I started a few years ago to gain more insights and knowledge in the world of music marketing and promotion. It features a collection of various mediums I've developed including journalism, photography, videography, podcast, etc.

KEY INSIGHTS

To get as many music lovers and event goers possible to enjoy the content. By creating great content we were able to use an in-bound marketing strategy to collect our audience information while providing them with quailty content and experiences. We made it a mission to give our audience the best music content, concert experiences, and insight on the best music. We partnered with several companies including The Howard Theatre, The Fillmore, Baltimore Soundstage, Rams Head Live, DJ Face, DJ Cuzzin B, Mark Infinite etc. to provide our followers with ticket giveaways, mixtapes, and more.

Users: 245K+
