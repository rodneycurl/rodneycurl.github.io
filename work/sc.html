CHALLENGE

How do you grow a community around a curated music and events website.

KEY INSIGHTS

Music lovers always enjoy great content and want to know what's new in their favorite genre's. People don't mind sharing their name and email for tickets to their favorite artist concert and Marketing Managers enjoy packing out their venue. Upon this discovery we made it our mission to give our audience the best music content, concert experiences, and insight on the best music. We partnered with several venues and influencers including The Howard Theatre, The Fillmore, Baltimore Soundstage, Rams Head Live, DJ Face, DJ Cuzzin B, Mark Infinite etc. to provide our followers with ticket giveaways, mixtapes, and more.

RESULTS 

Reach: 375K+ month
Users: 245K+
Video Views: 205K+ Countries
Press: Okayplayer, Black Jedi Zulu, Electric Llama, WHUR 96.3, and more.
