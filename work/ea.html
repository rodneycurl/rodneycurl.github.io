PROJECT

Electronic Arts Football Strategy →

ROLE

Strategist

CHALLENGE

Figure out how to connect the worlds of gamers and football fans. Culturally what do we bring forward to connect them?

KEY INSIGHTS

As Covid has pushed more people in the house, streaming has increased. There are significantly less Madden followers/viewers than other sports games (FIFA, NBA2K, etc.) on Twitch. EA has an opportunity to grow its engagement on streaming platforms by supporting the next Madden streamers. Streamers have key needs with respect to their setup and take pride in their channels making it a no brainer for EA to partner with the best and the brightest streamers.

RECOMMENDATION

EA could develop an aggressive new streamer program that will motivate ambitious Madden gamers to not only play the game they love but also document the game while amplifying the message of the game. EA could provide selected participants with gamer equipment (microphone, ring light, headset, controller, gamer chair, etc.) and an exclusive EA Madden Football Capsule box including a congratulatory letter, Madden swag, and more.
